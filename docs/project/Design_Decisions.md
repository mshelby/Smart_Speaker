# Design Decisions

**Overall Architecture**
- Three-part modular design: Barrel Module, Top Module, Bottom Module
- Barrel outer diameter increased to 192 mm to achieve target volume
- Top & bottom openings scaled to 56 mm radius (112 mm diameter)
- Wall thickness target: 2.5 mm after exterior dimpling
- Intentional 1 mm shadow gap preserved

**Bottom Module**
- Weight compartment optimized to wider diameter (~54–55 mm radius) and reduced height (9.0 – 9.5 mm)
- Top lid thinned to 2.5 mm
- 4 pegs to secure lid to weight cylinder
- Filled with steel shot + epoxy (~260–290g target)
- TPU sealing ring system retained

**Other Decisions**
- Full exterior dimpling on barrel
- Speaker mounting areas to be redesigned with thinner internal rings
- 4 magnets at 12/3/6/9 o’clock positions
- Light polyfill damping in main chamber
